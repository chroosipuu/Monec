Development Finance

In development finance advisory, we specialize in aiding small and medium developers with residential projects. Our expertise lies in our deep market knowledge and ability to match developers with the best lenders for their projects. However, our role goes beyond just connecting you with a lender; we act as your financial advisor throughout the entire development period, helping to ensure the financial success of your project.

Typically, financing can reach up to 50% of the Gross Development Value (GDV) or 70% of the project costs, though this may vary based on the specific project and lender.

LOAN SIZE

Ranges from 500K to 10M or more

LOAN PERIOD

Typically 12-24 months

INTEREST PAYMENTS

Monthly payments are preferred, but alternative arrangements like payment in kind or full bullet payments can be negotiated in certain cases

COLLATERAL

Secured by a first mortgage on the property

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

All necessary licenses must be current and in place

BORROWER

Should be a Spanish company, though non-Spanish beneficiaries are acceptable, Developer experience is a crucial factor for many loan providers

Bridge Lending

In bridge lending advisory, our primary focus is on delivering efficient financing solutions for high-end residential projects, such as luxury private houses and upscale apartments. However, our expertise extends to a wider range of projects, including apartment complexes and development land. Leveraging our extensive market knowledge, we tailor our services to each client's unique financial needs and match them with the most suitable lenders.

We handle a variety of financing scenarios, such as equity release, refinancing, bridging transactions, and funding pre-sale renovations or upgrades, along with addressing urgent financial needs or opportunities.

Financing is typically available for up to 50% of the property's value, though this may vary based on the specific project and lender. Our expertise is especially valuable in the high-end market, where customized financial solutions are essential.

LOAN SIZE

Ranges from 500K to 10M EUR or more

LOAN PERIOD

Usually between 6-24 months

INTEREST PAYMENTS

Monthly payments are preferred, but alternative arrangements like payment in kind or full bullet payments can be negotiated in certain cases

COLLATERAL

Secured with a first mortgage on the property

REGULATORY COMPLIANCE

All required licenses must be current and in place

BORROWER

Should be a Spanish company, though non-Spanish final beneficiaries are acceptable

About Us

Monec, led by managing partner Jaak Roosipuu, is built on a strong foundation in finance and banking. Jaak's career in financing began in 1992 with the co-founding of the Bank of Tallinn, which later became a part of the SEB Group. As head of the credit division, he built up the bank's lending business and had a key role in broadening its financing services throughout the region.

His career later evolved towards investment banking, leading to the establishment of Talinvest, a key player during Estonia's privatization era, and Suprema Securities, a significant entity in the Baltic regions’ corporate finance, brokerage, and asset management sectors. Suprema later became part of the Evli Group and the cornerstone of their Baltic operations.

Having resided in Marbella for the past 15 years, Jaak has developed an extensive network within the real estate sector. Combined with his extensive connections in the European and UK financial sectors, this network positions Monec as a valuable partner for clients in search of financing solutions and investors on the lookout for opportunities.

Our team is committed to leveraging our experience and networks to support our clients' success, offering reliable financing solutions and investment opportunities.
